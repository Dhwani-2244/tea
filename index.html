Our Story

Since 2015, ChaiNest has been crafting magical tea experiences in the heart of the city. What makes us special? Our centuries-old family recipes blended with local spices and served with authentic warmth that turns first-time visitors into lifelong regulars.

Every cup at ChaiNest tells a story - of aromatic spices handpicked from Kerala, Darjeeling leaves sourced directly from plantations, and the perfect froth that only comes from chai made with patience and love.
